# V16.8 — Teacher Schedule Change Notifications

- Owner schedule changes create notifications only for affected teachers.
- Added, modified, reassigned, and removed lessons are detected after successful main-data synchronization.
- Teachers and branch managers receive a real-time modal with change details.
- Acknowledged notifications do not appear again.
- Notification documents are separate from the main schedule data and do not alter scheduling, payroll, finance, permissions, or drag behavior.
